Property Overview

This stunning modern villa offers breathtaking ocean views from every level. Situated on a private cliffside in Malibu, this architectural masterpiece features floor-to-ceiling glass walls that perfectly frame the Pacific Ocean. Designed by award-winning architects, the property seamlessly blends luxury living with natural surroundings.

The open-concept living area features a gourmet kitchen with top-of-the-line appliances, a spacious dining area, and a living room with a modern fireplace. The primary suite is a true sanctuary with panoramic ocean views, a spa-like bathroom with a soaking tub, and a walk-in closet that rivals high-end boutiques.
