Our Mission

Redefining the luxury real estate experience since 2005

Founded in 2005 by industry veterans, Luxury Estates was born from a vision to transform the high-end real estate experience. We recognized that luxury clients deserve more than just transactions - they deserve a bespoke journey tailored to their unique aspirations.

Our mission is to elevate the luxury real estate experience through unparalleled market knowledge, innovative technology, and white-glove service. We combine global perspective with local expertise to deliver exceptional results for our discerning clients.

Our Journey

Milestones in our pursuit of excellence

2005

Founding of Luxury Estates

Founded by Olivia Richardson and Michael Chen with a vision to transform luxury real estate through exceptional service and market expertise.

2010

First International Office

Expanded to London, establishing our first international presence and marking the beginning of our global network.

2014

$1 Billion in Sales

Achieved the milestone of $1 billion in annual sales, cementing our position as a leader in luxury real estate.

2018

Technology Platform Launch

Introduced our proprietary digital platform, revolutionizing how clients discover and experience luxury properties.

2023

Global Presence

Now serving clients in 32 international locations with over 250 dedicated luxury property specialists.
